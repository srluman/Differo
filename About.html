About Differo

Hi, my name is Scot, and I am the founder of Differō.com. A few years ago I found myself in a place where I was questioning a lot of what I believed. I had a few experiences that began to challenge what I had so confidently sworn to be true and I need some people to talk to that were unbiased and could remain obective.

As I looked around me, I began to realize I was surrounded by other people just like me and that those I felt most comforttable with would be too emotionally involved to remain objective. Thanks to Twitter I got to know Martin Early who is the lead vocalist for The Ballroom Theives. Martin, and Atheist, and I, an Evangelical Christian, began emailing back anf forth talking about numerous difficult topics which choosing to mutually respect one another and keep an open mind.

I realized then that this was a huge need in the world. A place where people could learn from one another without the fear of being attacked. I needed a place where I could interact with people who were different than me but could maintain mutual respect instead of just seeking to argue. So I made one, and I call it "Differō"
